Why PickVault?

PickVault is a simple, clean, and free Android utility designed to take the impulse out of shopping. By providing a dedicated, permission-free sandbox to document, budget, and evaluate planned purchases, PickVault helps you make intentional acquisitions at your own pace without sharing your data.

Key Features

PickVault delivers an intentional space to track shopping contenders with high-utility features:

  • ✓

    Create custom collections ('Picks')

    Group items with custom targets, categories, budgets, and icons to structure your wishlists.

  • ✓

    Track granular details

    Record brands, price ranges, product URLs, notes, and research summaries for every logged item.

  • ✓

    Set preferred selections

    Highlight your top purchase contenders inside a collection to easily stay focused on what you need.

  • ✓

    100% offline local database

    Operates entirely locally on your device with zero permissions or internet connection needed.

  • ✓

    Completely free

    Enjoy basic utility with zero ads, third-party analytics trackers, or hidden charges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?
All list data, categories, and budgets are written directly to a secure local SQLite database on your Android device. It never leaves your phone and is not stored in the cloud.
How do I back up my picks?
Because PickVault does not request network access permissions, it cannot sync your data to cloud servers. To back up your lists, we recommend backing up your Android device using standard system backups or copy-archiving your app data.
Is the app really free?
Yes. PickVault is 100% free. There are no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no advertisements. It was created purely as a utility for mindful lifestyle planning.
Are you planning an iOS release?
Yes, we are currently planning a local SwiftUI version of PickVault for iOS that maintains the same offline, permission-free architecture.
